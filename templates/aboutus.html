ABOUT US

MyuSync - Finding your Musical Connection


If you're
(A) A musician willing to collaborate with other musicians or wanting to get hired, or
(B) A non-musician up for hiring, or
(C) A music lover,
you're at the right place!

What is MyuSync?

MyuSync is a one-stop platform to connect musicians with non-musicians and other musicians for collaboration. You may register as a Musician or a Non-Musician to access the amazing features from the comfort of your home!

Why MyuSync?

MyuSync solves all your problems of searching for people who might be interested in what you do. As a musician, you will be able to get hired by non-musicians and search for and collaborate with other musicians. As a non-musician, you will be able to search for and hire musicians. With our platform, you will have direct contact with genuinely interested people and there will be no involvement of any other person (so no middlemen, and no paying others to find someone for you!).
